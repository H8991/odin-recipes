Simple Salad Recipe

Description

A simple salad is a fresh, light dish typically consisting of mixed greens, vegetables, and a dressing. It's often served as a side or a light meal.

Home

Ingredients

  • Mixed greens: (e.g., lettuce, spinach, spring mix)
  • Vegetables: (e.g., cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell peppers, carrots)
  • Dressing: (e.g., olive oil, vinegar, salt, pepper, a squeeze of lemon juice)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Greens: Wash and thoroughly dry your mixed greens. This is crucial for the dressing to stick properly. You can use a salad spinner or pat them dry with paper towels. Tear or chop larger leaves into bite-sized pieces if needed.
  2. Chop the Vegetables: Wash and chop your chosen vegetables (e.g., halve cherry tomatoes, slice cucumber, dice bell peppers, grate carrots). Keep the pieces relatively uniform in size for easier eating.
  3. Combine Ingredients: Place the prepared greens and chopped vegetables in a large salad bowl.
  4. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl or jar, whisk together olive oil, vinegar (such as balsamic or red wine vinegar), salt, and pepper. Add a squeeze of fresh lemon juice if desired. Taste and adjust seasonings.
  5. Dress and Toss: Just before serving, drizzle the dressing over the salad ingredients. Gently toss the salad using tongs or your hands until everything is evenly coated. Be careful not to over-dress it.
  6. Serve Immediately: Serve your fresh, simple salad right away.
